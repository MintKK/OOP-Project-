What is the objective of regular volunteering programmes?

Regular volunteering programmes are meant to provide a route for youths who are looking for regular interaction with the beneficiaries. In addition, through trainings and interaction with beneficiaries, youth volunteers will be able to attain in-depth knowledge and skills related to volunteering and committing.



3. What is the commitment level like for the regular volunteering programmes?

What is the commitment level like for the regular volunteering programmes?

For effective engagement with the beneficiaries, there is a minimum of 6 months commitment. Members who sign up for the regular programmes are expected to commit to the sessions within the 6-month period. Members who wish to continue volunteering after the 6 months may do so by informing us.

7. Why do I have to record my hours?
X

Why do I have to record my hours?

So we can give you credit for your hard work! We can provide verification of hours to your school or employer, or any other organization that has a community service requirement. Knowing how many hours our volunteers donate is important as it allows us to quantify how much our volunteers contribute to our efforts, how much work our volunteers help us accomplish, and how much we rely on you!
